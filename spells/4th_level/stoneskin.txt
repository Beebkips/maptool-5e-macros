<br><font size=5><b>Stoneskin</b></font>
<br><font size=2>4th-level Abjuration</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Action
<br><b>Range:</b> Touch
<br><b>Components:</b> V, S, M (diamond dust worth 100 gp, which the spell consumes)
<br><b>Duration:</b> Concentration, up to 1 hour
<br>
This spell turns the flesh of a willing creature you touch as hard as stone. Until the spell ends, the target has resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage.

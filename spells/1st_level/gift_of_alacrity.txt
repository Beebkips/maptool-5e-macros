<br><font size=5><b>Gift of Alacrity</b></font>
<br><font size=2>1st-level Divination</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> 1 Minute
<br><b>Range:</b> Touch
<br><b>Components:</b> V, S
<br><b>Duration:</b> 8 hours
<br>
You touch a willing creature. For the duration, the target can add 1d8 to its initiative rolls.

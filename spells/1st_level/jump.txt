<br><font size=5><b>Jump</b></font>
<br><font size=2>1st-level Transmutation</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Action
<br><b>Range:</b> Touch
<br><b>Components:</b> V, S, M (a grasshopper's hind leg)
<br><b>Duration:</b> 1 minute
<br>
You touch a creature. The creature's jump distance is tripled until the spell ends.

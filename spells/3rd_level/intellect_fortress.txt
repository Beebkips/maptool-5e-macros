<br><font size=5><b>Intellect Fortress</b></font>
<br><font size=2>3rd-level Abjuration</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Action
<br><b>Range:</b> 30 feet
<br><b>Components:</b> V
<br><b>Duration:</b> Concentration, up to 1 hour
<br>
For the duration, you or one willing creature you can see within range has resistance to psychic damage, as well as advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.

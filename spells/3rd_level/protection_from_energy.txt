<br><font size=5><b>Protection from Energy</b></font>
<br><font size=2>3rd-level Abjuration</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Action
<br><b>Range:</b> Touch
<br><b>Components:</b> V, S
<br><b>Duration:</b> Concentration, up to 1 hour
<br>
For the duration, the willing creature you touch has resistance to one damage type of your choice: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder.

<br><font size=5><b>See Invisibility</b></font>
<br><font size=2>2nd-level Divination</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Action
<br><b>Range:</b> Self
<br><b>Components:</b> V, S, M (a pinch of Talc and a small sprinkling of powdered silver)
<br><b>Duration:</b> 1 hour
<br>
For the duration, you see invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible, and you can see into the Ethereal Plane. Ethereal creatures and objects appear ghostly and translucent.

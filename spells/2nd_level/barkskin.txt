<br><font size=5><b>Barkskin</b></font>
<br><font size=2>2nd-level Transmutation</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Action
<br><b>Range:</b> Touch
<br><b>Components:</b> V, S, M (a handful of oak bark)
<br><b>Duration:</b> Concentration, up to 1 hour
<br>
You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target's skin has a rough, bark-like appearance, and the target's AC can't be less than 16, regardless of what kind of armor it is wearing.

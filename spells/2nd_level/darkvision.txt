<br><font size=5><b>Darkvision</b></font>
<br><font size=2>2nd-level Transmutation</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Action
<br><b>Range:</b> Touch
<br><b>Components:</b> V, S, M (either a pinch of dried carrot or an agate)
<br><b>Duration:</b> 8 hours
<br>
You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has darkvision out to a range of 60 feet.

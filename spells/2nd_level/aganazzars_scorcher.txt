<br><font size=5><b>Aganazzar's Scorcher</b></font>
<br><font size=2>2nd-level Evocation</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Action
<br><b>Range:</b> 30 feet
<br><b>Components:</b> V, S, M (a red dragon's scale)
<br><b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
<br>
A line of roaring flame 30 feet long and 5 feet wide emanates from you in a direction you choose. Each creature in the line must make a Dexterity saving throw. A creature takes 3d8 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

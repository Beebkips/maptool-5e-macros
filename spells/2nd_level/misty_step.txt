<br><font size=5><b>Misty Step</b></font>
<br><font size=2>2nd-level Conjuration</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Bonus acn.
<br><b>Range:</b> Self
<br><b>Components:</b> V
<br><b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
<br>
Briefly surrounded by silvery mist, you teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space that you can see.

<br><font size=5><b>Magic Weapon</b></font>
<br><font size=2>2nd-level Transmutation</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Bonus acn.
<br><b>Range:</b> Touch
<br><b>Components:</b> V, S
<br><b>Duration:</b> Concentration, up to 1 hour
<br>
You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.

<br><font size=5><b>Sword Burst</b></font>
<br><font size=2>Cantrip-level Conjuration</font>
<br><b>Casting Time:</b> Action
<br><b>Range:</b> Self (5-foot radius)
<br><b>Components:</b> V
<br><b>Duration:</b> Instantaneous
<br>
You create a momentary circle of spectral blades that sweep around you. All other creatures within 5 feet of you must succeed on a Dexterity saving throw or take 1d6 force damageThis spell's damage increases by 1d6 when you reach 5th level (2d6), 11th level (3d6), and 17th level (4d6).
